Our Story

Jodhpur's Trusted Stationery Partner

Geeta Stationery has been serving the students, teachers, and offices of Jodhpur with quality stationery and school supplies for over 10 years. What started as a small neighbourhood shop has grown into one of Jodhpur's most trusted destinations for everything from school books to art supplies.

We believe every student deserves access to the best quality stationery at affordable prices. Our mission is simple — to be the one-stop shop that makes school and office supplies easy, accessible, and affordable for everyone in Jodhpur.

From Class 1 NCERT books to professional art supplies, toys, gifts, shoes, and print services — we carry thousands of products under one roof.
