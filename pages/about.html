Our Story

Jodhpur's Trusted Stationery Partner

Geeta Stationery has been serving the students, teachers, and offices of Jodhpur with quality stationery and school supplies for years. What started as a small neighbourhood shop has grown into one of Jodhpur's most trusted destinations for everything from school books to art supplies.

We believe every student deserves access to the best quality stationery at affordable prices. Our mission is simple — to be the one-stop shop that makes school and office supplies easy, accessible, and affordable for everyone.

From Class 1 NCERT books to professional art supplies, we carry thousands of products under one roof. Whether you're a student, teacher, artist, or office professional, Geeta Stationery has everything you need.
